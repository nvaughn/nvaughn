About Nathan

The Mat and the Desk

I built Gentle Warrior Yoga for a specific kind of person: the one who already knows how to perform, but needs help recovering. Leaders. Fathers. People who carry real weight and don't have time to waste on a practice that doesn't transfer.

My teaching blends breath-led movement, postural awareness, and the kind of deliberate stillness that most of us never let ourselves have. Every session is personal — built around where you are now, not where you think you should be.

I'm a Yoga Alliance RYT-200 certified instructor, registered September 2025. All are welcome. The mat doesn't check credentials.
